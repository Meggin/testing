# Course wrap-up

This concludes the final lesson. We have covered a lot of ground, but here are the main takeaways from this course:

 1. Think about profitability from the very beginning.
 1. Don't "fly blind"; use data and keep improving the way you collect and analyze it.
 1. Make sure you're charging the right users the right amount at the right time.
 1. Stay agile for new challenges and change as necessary.

Good luck &mdash; work hard, be nice, and make money!
